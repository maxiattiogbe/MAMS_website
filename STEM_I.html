STEM I

Overview

This class is taught by Dr. Crowthers. We work on a project in science, engineering or computer science from the beginning of the year through C term. We advance further if selected to participate in science fairs beyond the February fair that is required for everyone. I am working on a science project investigating how the fluorescence of a dye called acid red 94 (AR94) can be used to detect the concentration of lead ions (Pb2+) in water.

How can changes in the fluorescence emission intensity of acid red 94 (AR94) be used to detect and quantify Pb2+ ion concentration in water?

As Pb2+ concentration increases, the fluorescence emission intensity of an aqueous solution of AR94 decreases because the fluorescent dye AR94 associates with Pb2+ ions to form a complex that is not fluorescent.
